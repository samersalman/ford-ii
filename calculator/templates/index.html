FORD-II Non-Home Discharge Calculator

Estimates non-home discharge risk for adult trauma patients with fractures using the FORD-II score. The interface is designed to remain fully functional as a standard Flask form and then progressively enhanced with JavaScript.

NTDB 2019-2024 validation Held-out test n=650,737 AUROC 0.83

Enter the values available at presentation, calculate the score, then review the active contributors, raw versus clipped score, predicted probability, and observed event rates for the matched score band.

Research tool only. Do not substitute this output for clinician judgment.
